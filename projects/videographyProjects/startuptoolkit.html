The Start-up Toolkit

The Startup Toolkit was designed as a resource for early-stage companies to help them navigate their growth journey. As part of my role at HighTechXL, I collaborated with the team to identify key challenges faced by startups and curated a set of practical tools and materials to address those challenges. My responsibilities included researching industry best practices, creating content, and ensuring that the toolkit aligned with the needs of HighTechXL’s startups. I also played a role in the communication strategy, ensuring that the toolkit was effectively presented to stakeholders and that it became a go-to resource for HighTechXL's growing network of startups.
