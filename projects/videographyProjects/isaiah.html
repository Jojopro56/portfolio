Isaiah Crawford Basketball Hype Reel

This hype reel was part of a school assignment and required me to play with focus racking and manual focus. For this setup I used a Sony FX30 paired with a PDMovie Live Air wireless focus motor.

At the end there's a (at the time) trending "signature on lens" shot that I created with two magic arms and a clamp for the plexi glass to give the illusion that Isaiah is writing on the lens.
