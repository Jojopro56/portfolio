Short Film: "Tired"

Tired was part of a school assignment and required me to use some advanced methods of moving the camera. Think of a "Dolly forward while panning the camera to the right to follow a passing car" or "Mounting the camera on a fluidhead tripod to pan it towards different directions" which conveys a sequenced story within a single scene.

The movie is about a tired farmer coming home after a long work day longing for some rest. Throughout the evening he starts to notice his tiredness as peculiar things start to happen.

Sony FX30 with Neewer small lights for backlight
